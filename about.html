Our Story

Founded in 2020, eChannelling emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: healthcare should be accessible to everyone, regardless of location or time constraints. Our founders, a team of healthcare professionals and technology experts, recognized the challenges patients faced in booking appointments and accessing quality medical care.

Starting with just 5 hospitals and 50 doctors, we have grown to become Sri Lanka's largest healthcare appointment booking platform, serving over 45,000 patients and partnering with more than 500 qualified doctors across 50+ hospitals and medical centers.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our services, always keeping our core mission at heart: making healthcare accessible, affordable, and convenient for everyone.
