Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?
You can book appointments through our website or mobile app. Simply search for doctors, select your preferred time slot, and confirm your booking.
Can I cancel my appointment?
Yes, you can cancel appointments up to 2 hours before the scheduled time through your account dashboard or by calling our support team.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and online banking payments. Cash payments are also accepted at the hospital.
How do doctors join your platform?
Qualified doctors can apply through our doctor registration portal. Our team will verify credentials and approve applications within 3-5 business days.
