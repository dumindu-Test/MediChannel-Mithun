About National Hospital of Sri Lanka

The National Hospital of Sri Lanka is the largest hospital in the country and serves as the premier healthcare institution. Established in 1864, it has been providing comprehensive medical care to the nation for over 150 years.

As the flagship hospital of Sri Lanka's public healthcare system, we offer specialized medical services, advanced treatments, and serve as a teaching hospital for medical education. Our commitment is to provide quality healthcare accessible to all citizens regardless of their economic status.
